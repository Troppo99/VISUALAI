Seiketsu Streaming

In an AI-powered computer vision project, Seiketsu or Standardized focuses on monitoring organizational standards in factories. The AI evaluates workers' adherence to predefined arrangements, detecting deviations in tool and material placement to ensure a tidy, organized workplace. This improves consistency, reduces accidents, and boosts productivity while providing real-time feedback to help supervisors maintain compliance and promote discipline.
