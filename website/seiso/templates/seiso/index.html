This is Seiso Streaming

In the context of an AI computer vision project, Seiso refers to the detection of cleaning activities focused on specific objects within a factory setting. The AI system uses computer vision techniques to monitor and identify the cleaning of designated areas or objects, ensuring that these tasks are performed effectively and consistently. By analyzing video footage, the AI can track whether cleaning actions are being carried out as required, detecting any gaps or inefficiencies in the cleaning process. This not only helps maintain a cleaner and safer factory environment but also contributes to operational efficiency by providing real-time feedback and ensuring compliance with cleanliness standards.
